Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Tic-Tac-Toe collects, uses, and protects your data.

Information We Collect

  • Your Farcaster ID (FID)
  • Your Farcaster username (if available)
  • Your win/loss/draw counts for the leaderboard

How We Use Your Information

We use your FID and username to display your score on the global leaderboard. We do not share your data with third parties.

Data Retention

Leaderboard entries are stored until you request removal or the service is discontinued.

Security

We use Upstash Redis and secure hosting to store leaderboard data. Authentication is handled via Farcaster Quick Auth.
